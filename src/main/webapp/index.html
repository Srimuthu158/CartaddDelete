Products

In an e-commerce context, the "Products" section typically showcases items available for purchase. It serves as a catalog where customers can browse through various products, view details such as descriptions, prices, and images, and make selections based on their preferences and needs.

Cart

The "Cart" section is where selected products are temporarily stored before checkout. It displays a summary of items that a customer has added for purchase, including quantities, prices, and sometimes options for modifications like quantity adjustments or removal of items. It provides a convenient way for customers to review their selections before proceeding to payment.

Wishlist

The "Wishlist" section allows users to save products they are interested in but are not ready to purchase immediately. It serves as a personal collection or reminder of items that caught their attention. Customers often use wishlists to track items they plan to buy later or to share gift ideas with others.
